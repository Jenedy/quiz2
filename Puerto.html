Puerto Princesa, Palawan Philippines Princess Eulalia Park, Iwahig Firefly Watching, Tabon Caves, Cave Systems, Underground River, Honda Bay, Biodiversity, etc. Puerto Princesa, located in Palawan, Philippines, is a captivating city known for its natural wonders and commitment to environmental preservation. It's home to the world-famous Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New7Wonders of Nature, featuring an awe-inspiring subterranean river winding through limestone caves.
