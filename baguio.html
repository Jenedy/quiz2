Baguio

•DESTINATION •UNIQUE FEATURES •BRIEF DESCRIPTION
Baguio City, Philippines Burnham Park, Experience the Baguio Night Market, Go strawberry picking, Explore the BenCab Museum, Take a stroll along Session Road, Take a scenic drive to nearby places, etc. Baguio City, located in the Philippines, is a popular tourist spot known for its cool climate, picturesque mountain landscapes, and vibrant local culture. Visitors can explore lush parks, shop for unique handicrafts, and enjoy a variety of outdoor activities. The city is also famous for its annual Panagbenga Festival, which features colorful parades and street dancing. Baguio offers a diverse range of experiences, from cultural immersion to natural beauty, making it a delightful destination for travelers.
