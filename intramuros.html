Intramuros, Manila, Philippines Horse-Drawn Carriage Ride, Walking Tour, Intramuros Light and Sound Museum, Attend Festivals, Climb the Walls, Photography, etc. Intramuros is a historic and captivating tourist spot in Manila, Philippines. It's a walled city that dates back to Spanish colonial times, offering a glimpse into the country's rich history and culture. Visitors can explore centuries-old architecture, forts like Fort Santiago, stunning churches, and charming cobblestone streets. It's a place to immerse yourself in the Philippines' colonial past, enjoy cultural activities, and savor local cuisine while strolling through picturesque plazas.
